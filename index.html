AP Music Theory

This course provides a comprehensive foundation in music theory as required for AP-level studies. From basic notation to harmonic analysis, you'll learn everything you need to succeed.

Unit 1: Music Notation

  • Staff, clefs, and note values
  • Time signatures and rhythm basics
  • Pitch and accidentals

Unit 2: Scales and Key Signatures

  • Major and minor scales
  • Circle of fifths
  • Key signature identification

Unit 3: Intervals and Chords

  • Simple and compound intervals
  • Triads and seventh chords
  • Roman numeral analysis

Unit 4: Harmonic Progressions

  • Cadences and common chord progressions
  • Chord inversions and voice leading
  • Part writing techniques

Unit 5: Form and Analysis

  • Binary and ternary forms
  • Phrase structure and musical sentences
  • Score reading and analysis

AP Music Appreciation

Explore the history, genres, and cultural impact of music through the ages. This course is designed to build a deep understanding and love for music from all eras and around the world.

Unit 1: Elements of Music

  • Melody, harmony, rhythm, texture
  • Dynamics, timbre, and form

Unit 2: Music Through the Ages

  • Medieval and Renaissance music
  • Baroque, Classical, and Romantic periods
  • 20th century to contemporary music

Unit 3: World Music

  • Music traditions from Africa, Asia, and Latin America
  • Folk vs. classical in various cultures

Unit 4: Popular Music & Media

  • Jazz, rock, hip hop, electronic music
  • Music in film, TV, and games
  • Cultural and social influence of music

Unit 5: Musical Analysis and Reflection

  • Listening skills and guided analysis
  • Reflective writing and response activities
